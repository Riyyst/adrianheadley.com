Projects

A couple of current projects and ideas I’m working on. The platform continues to evolve, with new tools and improvements being added to enhance everyday usability.

R

Riyst.com

Riyst.com is a utility site that collects everyday tools and useful websites into one place. The goal is to make it quick and simple to get to the things you use most often without hunting through bookmarks.

  • Curated links to practical tools and resources.
  • Designed as a simple “launchpad” for day-to-day tasks.
  • Built to evolve over time as new tools are added.
Visit Riyst.com
CF

ChurchFinder

ChurchFinder is a longer-term project to surface information about churches across the UK – making it easier for people to discover where they might feel at home, whether they’re local or visiting somewhere new.

  • Focus on clear information: location, denomination, and key details.
  • Built with the idea of mapping and search at its core.
  • Early-stage work in progress, with plenty of room to grow.
Visit ChurchFinder.uk
OC

Russian Orthodox Calendar

Russian Orthodox Calendar is a daily-updating website that uses live date and calendar data to present an aesthetically pleasing liturgical calendar for the Russian Orthodox tradition. Each day’s view highlights the key commemorations and provides a clear sense of where you are in the church year.

  • Shows daily commemorations, fasting rules, and feast days in one place.
  • Provides daily scripture readings and prayers to support regular devotion.
  • Includes the option to listen to Orthodox chants while you read, reflect, or pray.
Visit Russian Orthodox Calendar
